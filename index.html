About

An advisory practice and
intellectual platform

The Leading Standard is an independent platform dedicated to leadership, governance, and integrity in international sport. Rather than functioning as a traditional consulting firm, it operates as a trusted advisor and intellectual platform — engaging selectively with organisations facing complex governance challenges or pursuing institutional reform.

The platform combines senior advisory work with thought leadership and strategic reform initiatives. This hybrid model allows it to address the needs of individual organisations while also contributing to systemic improvements in governance standards across international sport.

Mandates are undertaken selectively to ensure senior-level involvement and genuine independence.

The Hybrid Model

Three complementary pillars

The Leading Standard combines advisory work, intellectual contribution and strategic initiatives to address governance challenges at both the organisational and systemic level.

I

Senior Advisory Practice

Independent strategic advice to leaders and organisations in international sport — from governance reviews and institutional reform to crisis advisory and safeguarding architecture. Engagements are selective and confidential.

II

Thought Leadership

Analysis and publications on institutional challenges in international sport — governance reform, safeguarding frameworks, the independence of ethics systems, and the foundations of institutional legitimacy and stakeholder trust.

III

Strategic Reform Initiatives

Collaborative projects aimed at strengthening governance and integrity systems across sport — from benchmarking initiatives and safeguarding frameworks to governance dialogue forums.
